Hi, welcome to Loft in Space. We are a husband and wife team, living in Australia with our miniature dachshund. We are passionate about building innovative and socially responsible software.

We like to collaborate with and help smart people on projects that make a positive difference to communities and the environment.

Jeremy Mawson
Developer, Solution Designer & Iteration Manager
| | | | |

Jeremy started programming in the mid 1980s and has been fascinated with building software ever since. He has been working in technology since the mid 1990s and has dedicated much of this time to developing software using agile methodologies.

He is keenly interested in the pursuit of quality through automated testing techniques and functional programming. He enjoys learning from and sharing his knowledge with development teams on these topics and more. His programming experience includes many languages in various industries developing web-based and complex back-end systems.

Jeremy's strengths are in crafting software that meets business objectives in a pragmatic and efficient manner and improving software development, through the introduction and implementation of lightweight tooling and processes.

Chen Li
Product Manager, Project Manager
& Business Analyst
|

Chen has a Chinese background and a master's degree in engineering. She's worked in technology since the late 90s. Her work ranges from product management, project management, analysis, testing, and team management.

Her interest lies in strategic analysis, developing business and product plans, product functional design, as well as evaluating business and product benefits/performance/costs in order to deliver changes in the most efficient way.

Chen's strengths are in conceptualising complex requirements, simplifying and clearly defining them just on the right level for problem solving. Her strong technical background combined with commercial acumen enables her to collaborate with both technical and business professionals to develop fit-for-purpose solutions that are aligned with business strategies.
