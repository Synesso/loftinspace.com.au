Hi, and welcome to Loft in Space. We are a husband and wife team, living in Australia with our miniature dachshund. We met in the late 90's while working together on e-commence payment solutions. We are passionate about building innovative and socially responsible software.
We like to collaborate (or work together) with smart people on projects that make a positive difference to communities and the environment.

Jeremy Mawson
Developer, Solution Designer and Blah blah

Developer, Solution Designer & Iteration Manager

Jeremy started programming in the mid 1980s when he received a Commodore 16 for his birthday. He has been fascinated with building software ever since. He has been working in technology since the mid 1990s and has dedicated much of this time to developing software using agile methodologies.

He is keenly interested in the pursuit of quality through automated testing techniques and functional programming. He enjoys learning from and sharing his knowledge with development teams on these topics and more. His programming experience includes many languages in banking and various other industries developing web-based and complex back-end systems.

Jeremy's strengths are in crafting software that meets business objectives in a pragmatic and efficient manner and improving development and build processes, through the introduction and implementation of new tooling and processes .
